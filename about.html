About The Workshop App

The Workshop App is a web app designed to help in a workshop during the session. Step by step the participants can follow the workshop at their own pace because they get all the steps in the app.

You can get and deploy your own versio of The Workshop App from its GitHub repository.

Github: https://github.com/kikeelectronico/The-Workshop-App
